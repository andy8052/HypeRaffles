Hype Raffles

This is Hype Raffles, a blockchain based system for provably fair raffles

This site requires metamask. We recommend you install it.

About

Hype Raffles is created to facilite fair and low cost raffles for clothing and sneakers. I noticed a growth in raffles for different items from clothing companies like Supreme and Palace. Ethereum smart contracts are an ideal way to augment these raffles to make them provably fair for their entrants and to allow for easy creation, payout and managing by raffle creators.

How It Works

Hype Raffles is based on a Solidity smart contract that is deployed on the Ethereum blockchain. The smart contract allows for raffle creators to generate raffles and link them to instagram posts to house photos of the item up for raffle. From there, users can view open raffles, the history of their creators and the raffle logistics. These raffles are immutable on the blockchain.

Why Use Crypto?

Systems such as paypal and venmo have serveral major flaws.
1. These systems require the money to go into the hands of the raffle creator prior to raffle completion. This allows for people to just cancel the raffle and leave with peoples money. The Hype Raffle smart contract acts as an escrow contract for these raffles.
2. This contract allows for an immutable history of raffle creators history. If a seller does not have a good history of raffles, you can see that publicly.

Fees

Paypal charges 4% fees on invoices. Hype Raffles only take a 2% cut of all raffle proceeds and slowly scale down to 0.5% of fees based on the number of raffles you have completes. This is designed to encourage trustworthy raffle creators.
